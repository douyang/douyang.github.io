About

Physician-scientist and statistician with focus on cardiology, cardiovascular imaging, deep learning, machine learning, and the statistical analysis of large datasets. I majored in Statistics at Rice University, obtained my MD at UCSF, and recieved post-graduate medical education in internal medicine and cardiology at Stanford University. I am mentored by Euan Ashley and James Zou.
